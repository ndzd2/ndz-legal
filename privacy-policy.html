Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the ndz.ai social media tool ("the App") handles information.

1. About the App

The App is a personal tool used solely by its owner to publish content to social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. It is not available to the general public.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. The App accesses only the owner's own social media accounts using official platform APIs.

3. Use of Platform APIs

The App uses TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube APIs to publish content on behalf of the account owner. No data from these platforms is stored beyond what is necessary for authentication (access tokens stored locally on the owner's device).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

5. Security

All credentials and tokens are stored locally and are never transmitted to any third-party server.
